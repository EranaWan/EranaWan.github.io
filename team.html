Who We Are

Team

Tim He (CEO) sold enough Coca-Cola in 6th grade to buy a new laptop and that’s when he fell in love with entrepreneurship. Since then, he has started an incubator for startups and helped raise funds for a Fintech. This background in entrepreneurship, coupled with his studies in real estate, have led him to Arc, to which he is fully committed and treats as his full-time job.

Min Su Kim (COO) has a tireless dedication to duty that translates to an altruistic desire to help others. Her backgrounds in Accounting, Sociology, and Law inform her abilities to channel these passions, which has translated into her work at the Civil Rights Justice Center and a non-profit health clinic, where she serves marginalized groups. She joined Arc in recognition of the real-world problem that it seeks to solve.

Erana Wan (CIO) is a creative genius who applies her analytic backgrounds in Economics and Math to visualizing and plotting outside of the box ideas. She’s helped Arc to incorporate Figma, Photoshop, CSS, and other design technologies to create mockups that allow interviewees to visualize our product. Erana is also largely responsible for the extensive customer research that we have.

Daniel Simic (CFO) graduated in Finance and Entrepreneurship, is currently working at a commercial real estate agency, and has worked in similar roles at prior startups. He has a thorough understanding of the lay of the land, and enjoys exploring the financials of organizations, how that impacts their operations, and vice versa. Daniel also has a talent for relating to people and establishing connections through his great sense of humor.

Amina Ahmed (CTO) draws on her educational background in Management Information Systems and marketing and coding experience to seamlessly bridge the gap between the business and technology sides of Arc. In her personal life and beyond, she devotes herself to improving the lives of those around her. In doing so, Amina helps us to redefine our purpose, as she never fails to relate her personal purpose to her work.

Nathan Yan (Backend Developer) studies and breathes all things computer science. He has experience in full stack development using React, ReactNative, Express/NodeJS, MongoDB and Python. He taught math and programming at intermediate and gifted levels for elementary through high school and was a student astrophysics researcher at Yale.

Advisors

Sara Weaver is a real estate developer and investor. She has 20 years of experience overseeing new property acquisitions and managing her existing portfolio, as well as 11 years of experience as a managing broker. Sara is always eager to help young entrepreneurs and has been on the UW Buerk Center Advisory Board for 15 years, and we appreciate both her entrepreneurial and real estate backgrounds.

Mariah Gentry is the founder of Mariah Gentry Photography, NW Budget Brides, Wild Wing Studios, and Joey Bra. She loves teaching entrepreneurship and working with small businesses by offering boutique online marketing and strategy services to help them continue to scale.

John Anderson, M.SE, M.BA, D.IT (ABD) built custom real-time decisioning systems used by 40K representatives to combat churn in the wireless industry. He managed a team of 22 technical specialists supporting marketing and analytic systems, leveraging one of the largest operational customer data repositories in the world.

Dan Keller, M.Ed is highly committed to the safety of his colleagues. He is nationally recognized as being in the top 1% of mortgage loan officers, speaks at the largest real estate conferences, and teaches students as an affiliate professor at the UW Runstad Department of Real Estate.

Vincent Lucero has worked in technical managerial positions at both Fortune 10 companies and bootstrapped startups. He has also built a Fintech company that provides loans to underserved communities, as well as a video game that was funded by Google executives and one of Yelp’s founders.

Kristine Marzolf is a business veteran with 30 years of experience in marketing, business development, and product management. She is one of the Top 20 Startup Coaches, Top 50 Female C-Level Marketing Executives in Seattle, and Top 80 Twitter Accounts for Startups.

Alan Leong, M.SE, PhD, a beloved lecturer at the UW Buerk Center, is well-acquainted with several venture capital spheres and communities throughout Silicon Valley and Seattle. Over 150 companies call him advisor and he has been a personal mentor to several members of Arc for several years..
